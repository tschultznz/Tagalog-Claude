STAGE 4B PHRASEBANK — v2 Claude-patched
Last updated: 2026-05-09

PURPOSE
This file gives the tutor high-value practical chunks.
It is an internal selection bank, not an answer key.

PHRASEBANK USE RULE
Do not paste this file raw into drills.
Do not give Tom phrase + English meaning as a full answer key before he produces.
Select useful chunks, then create production prompts with short hints.

LABELS
[CORPUS] = supported by Glossika corpus
[ADD-ON] = high-frequency useful addition
[TAGLISH-NATURAL] = normal code-switched speech in many PH contexts
[PO-REGISTER] = polite/respectful use
[CASUAL] = casual with friends/partner
[NEUTRAL] = safe general speech
[NATURAL TARGET] = preferred spoken target over stilted corpus phrasing

==================================================
1. SERVICE / REPAIR MAINTENANCE
==================================================

[NEUTRAL]
Kailan ko ito puwedeng kunin?
- frame: kailan ko ito puwedeng + verb
- use: ask pickup time

[NEUTRAL]
Hindi pa ito tapos.
- frame: hindi pa + pronoun + state
- use: not finished yet

[NEUTRAL]
Kailan matatapos ito?
- frame: kailan + ma-future/result + ito
- use: ask when it will be finished

[NEUTRAL]

- frame: actor verb + na lang ba + ako
- use: should I just wait here

[NEUTRAL]
Balikan ko na lang ba ito mamaya?
- frame: target verb + ko + na lang ba + object + time
- use: should I just come back for this later

[NEUTRAL]

- use: ask for status update

[NEUTRAL]

- frame: ibigay + ang thing + sa person
- use: give the thing to someone
